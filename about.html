Our Project

Naturelle is a beauty and skincare project designed to blend natural aesthetics with modern web design.

From skin care to makeup essentials, Naturelle is about celebrating natural beauty while embracing creativity in design and technology.

Key Features

Product Filtering

Users can filter products by category, making shopping easy and intuitive.

Ribbons & Promotions

Special ribbons highlight promotions and limited-time offers to catch users' attention.

Old Price Display

Exclusive discounted products show the original price crossed out, clearly indicating savings.

Quantity Control

Users can adjust product quantities directly on the card with increment/decrement buttons.

Equal Height Cards & Image Fit

All product cards have uniform height, and images are resized to fit consistently.

Cart Integration

Products can be added to the cart with instant feedback using SweetAlert popups.

User Login & Authentication

Users must log in to access products and manage their cart. Data is stored in localStorage for persistence.

Secure Login & Admin Access

Only admins can access the "Add Product" page, preventing unauthorized modifications.

Admin Remove Button

Registered admins can remove products directly from the products page. This button is hidden for regular users to prevent unauthorized deletions.
